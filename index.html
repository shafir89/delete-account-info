Account Deletion Information

Users can delete their accounts directly inside our mobile application. To delete your account, open the app and go to:

  • Profile / Settings
  • Select Delete Account
  • Confirm the deletion

Once confirmed, the app sends a secure request to our backend API to permanently remove your account and all associated data.

If you can't access the app

You may request account deletion by contacting: support@domainmu.com

Your account will be deleted within 7 days according to our data policy.
